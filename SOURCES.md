### Cultural Sensitivity

When researching and discussing the Nian legend:
- Recognize it as living cultural tradition, not mere folklore
- Respect the significance of Chinese New Year to billions of people
- Acknowledge the diversity of regional variations
- Understand the connection between legend and contemporary practice

## Research Notes

### Primary Themes
1. **Origin Myth**: Explains the "why" behind New Year traditions
2. **Community Protection**: Emphasizes collective action and shared knowledge
3. **Cultural Continuity**: Traditions passed through generations
4. **Symbolic Power**: Colors, sounds, and lights as protective forces

### Modern Relevance
- The Nian legend remains taught to children worldwide
- New Year traditions directly reference the legend
- Cultural tourism incorporates Nian themes
- Contemporary art and media continue to adapt the story
